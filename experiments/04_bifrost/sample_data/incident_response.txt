Incident Response Guide

The first responder should confirm the user-visible symptom, identify the affected service, and check whether the issue is ongoing. The response should prioritize customer impact over internal ownership debates.

Severity 1 incidents affect production availability, data integrity, or security. They require an incident commander, a communications lead, and a technical lead.

Useful debugging steps include checking recent deploys, error rates, latency percentiles, dependency health, queue depth, and database saturation.

During mitigation, prefer reversible actions such as rollback, traffic shifting, feature flag disablement, or rate limiting. Avoid speculative changes that expand the blast radius.

After resolution, the team writes a post-incident review with a timeline, root cause, contributing factors, detection gaps, and concrete follow-up actions.
